# Personal Brand Website for Allison Krinsky (@itsallykrinsky)

## Project Overview

Build a modern, aspirational personal brand website for Allison Krinsky - a Product Manager at JPMorgan Chase working on firmwide data & AI platforms. The website should serve as the digital home for her content brand focused on empowering ambitious young women in tech and career development.

## Brand Identity

### Core Positioning
- **Tone:** Direct and unapologetic "big sister" energy ‚Äî tough love, no fluff, telling people what they *need* to hear
- **Style:** Aspirational but grounded ‚Äî "If she can do it, so can I"
- **Core Values:** Ambition, intentionality, independence, balance

### Mission
To inspire ambitious young women to strategically design lives they love, leveraging career, intentional routines, and self-development as tools for growth while showing them what's possible through a transparent, multidimensional lens.

### Vision
To be a leading voice for early-career women who want more than "just a job" ‚Äî they want purpose, independence, and the ability to build their future with intention.

## Target Audience

**Primary:** U.S.-based women, 18‚Äì25, college students & early career professionals
- Highly ambitious with big career and lifestyle goals
- Motivated by self-improvement, financial independence, and career growth
- Looking for "learning how to adult" in a strategic way
- Want lived career advice they won't hear from parents or professors
- Desire a blend of *inspiration* and *practical strategy*

**Key Emotion:** Visitors should leave feeling capable and energized to take action in their own lives

## Technical Requirements

### Design Principles
- **Single Page Layout:** Clean, scrollable one-pager with smooth navigation
- **Modern & Minimal:** Professional but not corporate, aspirational but grounded
- **Mobile-First:** Primary audience browses on mobile
- **Fast Loading:** Optimized images and performance
- **Accessible:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant

### Color Palette Suggestions
- Primary: Sophisticated neutrals (warm blacks, creams, taupes)
- Accent: Subtle golds or deep burgundy for aspirational touch
- Keep it feminine but professional - avoid overly cutesy

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif for headers
- Readable serif or refined sans-serif for body text
- Strong hierarchy that guides the eye

## Voice & Copy Guidelines

### Do's:
- Be direct and honest
- Use "you" to speak directly to reader
- Share specific, actionable insights
- Show confidence without arrogance
- Make complex topics accessible
- Inspire action

### Don'ts:
- No corporate jargon or buzzwords
- Avoid being preachy or condescending
- Don't oversell or hype
- No fake modesty or excessive humility
- Avoid generic motivational platitudes

### Example Copy Angles:
- "I'm a Product Manager building AI platforms at JPMorgan ‚Äî and I'm here to give you the career advice no one else will."
- "Strategic living isn't about perfection. It's about intention."
- "Everything I share comes from actually doing it, not just thinking about it."
